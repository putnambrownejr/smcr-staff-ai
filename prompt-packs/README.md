# Prompt Packs — AI-Ready Guides for Marines

These are **copy-paste prompt packs** — each one turns any AI chat (ChatGPT, Claude,
Gemini, Copilot, etc.) into a specialized Marine Corps advisor for that topic.

## How to Use

1. **Pick the pack** that matches what you need help with
2. **Open your AI** — ChatGPT, Claude.ai, Gemini, or whatever you use
3. **Copy the entire file** and paste it as your first message (or upload the `.md` file)
4. **Ask your question** — the AI now has the context to give you Marine-specific answers

That's it. No coding, no installation, no terminal commands.

## Tips

- **Be specific.** Instead of "help me with drill," say "I'm an 0302 Captain reporting
  to a new infantry company for my first drill this Saturday. What do I need to prepare?"
- **Give context.** The more detail you provide (rank, MOS, unit type, situation), the
  better the AI's response will be.
- **Verify everything.** These packs cite real references (MCOs, MARADMINs, joint pubs),
  but policies change. Always confirm against current official sources before acting.

## Security

**UNCLASSIFIED only.** Do not paste classified information, CUI, COMSEC, real frequencies,
call signs, sensitive operational details, or unnecessary PII into any AI chat.

All outputs are **advisory drafts** — they require human review and are not official
Marine Corps guidance.
