# Doctrine Reference

Quick lookup for USMC and joint doctrinal publications by topic. Maps functional
areas to publication numbers and explains what each governs.

## Publication Hierarchy

| Tier | Prefix | Purpose | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Capstone** | MCDP | Foundational philosophy and warfighting concepts | MCDP 1 Warfighting |
| **Warfighting** | MCWP | How to execute warfighting functions | MCWP 3-31 Fires and Effects |
| **Tactics/Techniques** | MCTP | Detailed procedures and staff processes | MCTP 3-30A MAGTF Staff Training |
| **Reference** | MCRP | Reference data, organizational information | MCRP 1-10.1 Organization of the USMC |
| **Joint** | JP | Joint doctrine across services | JP 2-0 Joint Intelligence |

## Capstone Doctrine (MCDP)

| Publication | Title | What it governs |
|---|---|---|
| **MCDP 1** | Warfighting | Marine Corps philosophy — maneuver warfare, combined arms, mission tactics, commander's intent. The intellectual foundation for everything else. |
| **MCDP 1-0** | Marine Corps Operations | MAGTF employment across the range of military operations. Defines MAGTF structure (CE/GCE/ACE/LCE). |
| **MCDP 1-3** | Tactics | Application of combat power to defeat an enemy at a particular time and place. |
| **MCDP 2** | Intelligence | Doctrinal foundation for Marine intelligence. |
| **MCDP 3** | Expeditionary Operations | MEF/MEB/MEU employment. Echeloned deployment, 60-day sustainment, naval integration. |
| **MCDP 4** | Logistics | Marine logistics philosophy. Sustainment as a warfighting function. |
| **MCDP 6** | Command and Control | Theory and philosophy of C2 — reaching effective decisions faster than an adversary. |

## Force Design and Modernization Documents

These are not traditional doctrine but shape current force employment:

| Document | What it governs |
|---|---|
| **Force Design 2030** (and annual updates) | Structural transformation — distributed littoral ops, stand-in forces, MLR, divestiture of legacy formations, loitering munitions, sUAS, EW/SIGINT, counter-sUAS. |
| **Installations and Logistics 2030** | Contested sustainment, diversified distribution, installation readiness. |
| **Training and Education 2030** | Modernized training environment, LVC, AI literacy, integrated kill webs, unmanned systems. |
| **Talent Management 2030** | Personnel reform — talent alignment, retention, lateral moves, technical skill management. |

## Notes

- **Publication numbers change.** MCOs get revised, MCWPs get new edition letters. Verify against the [MCPEL](https://www.marines.mil/News/Publications/MCPEL/) before treating a number as current.
- **UNCLASSIFIED only.** All publications referenced here are publicly available through the MCPEL or official .mil sites.
- **Doctrine lags experimentation.** Current force structure and capabilities may not yet be reflected in published doctrine, especially for Force Design formations like the MLR.
