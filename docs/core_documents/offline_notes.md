## Working Purpose

This project has three connected purposes:

1. **Chief of staff / aide support for SMCR use**
   - drill rhythm awareness
   - travel and DTS continuity
   - reminder and suspense management
   - career and PME awareness
   - automation-friendly continuity between drills

2. **A functional advisory staff bench**
   - section-specific staff support
   - round-robin staff review
   - training, planning, and estimate support
   - staff-product generation and cleanup
   - stronger reserve-specific continuity for thin-bench use

3. **A usable local dashboard**
   - task management
   - quick reference access
   - local document organization
   - reusable workflow entry points
