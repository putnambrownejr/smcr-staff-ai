## Expected Uses

- `doctrine-opord-assistant`
- Staff planning checklists
- Exercise planning
- MCPP templates and prompts
- R2P2 education and compressed-planning guardrails
- TDG and wargaming support for commander decision points

## Notes

The OPORD assistant should produce templates, checklists, and training-only drafts. It should distinguish
between:

- deliberate MCPP for new, complex, or poorly rehearsed problems
- compressed R2P2-style refinement for familiar, SOP-backed, time-constrained problems

Real operational plans, exact movements, current unit missions, and sensitive locations should be refused
or reduced to generic planning education.
