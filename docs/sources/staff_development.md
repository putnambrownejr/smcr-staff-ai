they should cite. The target is not a manual voice. The target is a staff officer who understands schoolhouse
fundamentals and can still make hard choices under reserve friction.

## Notes

Use this source set to tune:

- decisiveness
- commander decision framing
- cross-staff synchronization
- practicality under time pressure
- willingness to cut weak ideas

Do not use these sources to imitate biography, private speech, or invented personal anecdotes. The aim is
professional judgment and staff maturity, not cosplay.
