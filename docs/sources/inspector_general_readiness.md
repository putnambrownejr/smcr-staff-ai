# Inspector General And Inspection Readiness Grounding

This note captures official public grounding for IG-adjacent readiness, inspections, referral boundaries, and complaint-channel discipline. It is a reference note, not a copied order.

## Official Sources

- Inspector General of the Marine Corps
  - Official URL: https://www.igmc.marines.mil/
  - Use for: IGMC mission, independence, inspections, assistance, assessments, and investigation posture.
  - Notes: the best current public doorway for IG program context.

- MCO 5430.1A W/ADMIN CH-2
  - Official URL: https://www.marines.mil/News/Publications/MCPEL/Electronic-Library-Display/Article/4062347/mco-54301a-wadmin-ch-2/
  - Use for: Marine Corps Inspector General Program authorities, roles, relationships, and independence boundaries.
  - Notes: primary grounding for what belongs in IG channels and what does not.

- MCO 5370.8A W/ADMIN CH-1
  - Official URL: https://www.marines.mil/News/Publications/MCPEL/Electronic-Library-Display/Article/1924487/mco-53708a/
  - Use for: Hotline Program, fraud/waste/abuse/mismanagement reporting, and complaint-channel boundaries.
  - Notes: good grounding when a staff problem starts drifting toward a complaint issue.

- IGMC Functional Area Checklists
  - Official URL: https://www.igmc.marines.mil/Divisions/Inspections-Division/Checklists/
  - Use for: inspection readiness, functional-area evidence, and current checklist discipline.
  - Notes: useful for turning vague readiness complaints into concrete inspection-prep work.

## Expected Agent Usage

- inspection readiness plan
- inquiry boundary note
- readiness trend memo
- referral-path reminder

## Safety / Freshness Notes

- IG independence matters. Do not use this note to shortcut complaints, investigations, or formal referrals.
- Local command IG, SJA, safety, and chain-of-command responsibilities may overlap around facts, but not in the same role.
