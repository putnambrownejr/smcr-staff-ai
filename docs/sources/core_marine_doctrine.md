These are key public, official Marine Corps doctrine references for future RAG, staff planning, PME, leadership, information, intelligence, and tactics workflows. Store links and metadata only; do not commit full publication text.

- Category: Foundational doctrine

## Safety And Usage Notes

Use these references for doctrine-grounded education, planning templates, fictional/training scenarios, PME prompts, and staff process support. Do not process classified information, CUI, real current operations, current movements, sensitive unit plans, targeting, or protected operational details.

When connected to RAG later, responses should cite publication title, official MCPEL URL, retrieval date, version/status, and section/page metadata when available.
