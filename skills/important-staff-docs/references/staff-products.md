## Critical Mockups

### WARNO

```text
1. Situation: What changed or may change.
2. Mission: Likely task and purpose.
3. Initial tasks: Who starts planning or prep now.
4. Timeline: Planning events, suspense, decision point.
5. Coordinating instructions: RFIs, constraints, next update.
```

### OPORD / CONOP

```text
1. Situation: enemy/threat, friendly, attachments, terrain/weather, civil considerations.
2. Mission: who, what, when, where, why.
3. Execution: intent, concept, tasks, coordinating instructions, assessment.
4. Sustainment: supply, movement, maintenance, medical, personnel/admin.
5. Command and Signal: command relationships, CPs, succession, reports, PACE.
```

### FRAGO

```text
Reference: base order and serial.
Changes: only what changed.
Tasks: updated task/purpose by unit or staff section.
Coordinating instructions: new timing, control measures, reports, constraints.
Unchanged: state what remains in effect when ambiguity is likely.
```

### SITREP

```text
Current status: friendly posture and significant changes.
Operations: what is happening now.
Personnel/casualties: only required status detail.
Logistics: supply, equipment, movement, maintenance, medical friction.
Communications: C2 or reporting issues.
Next 24-72: expected activity, decisions, support requirements.
```

## Review Checks

- Does the product answer who, what, when, where, why, and so what?
- Does each section have an owner, reviewer, and suspense?
- Are assumptions labeled instead of briefed as facts?
- Does the base order avoid duplicating annex detail?
- Are special-staff reviews visible before execution?
- Is the product unclassified, training-safe, and stripped of unnecessary PII?
