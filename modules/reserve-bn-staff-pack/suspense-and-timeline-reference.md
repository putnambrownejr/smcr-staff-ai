| Mid-cycle counseling              | Halfway through reporting period |

## Reserve-Specific Suspense Gotchas

1. **The 28-day gap kills admin chains.** If you don't close an action before releasing Marines on Sunday, it probably won't get done until next drill. Put a suspense on it.

2. **I&I can only do so much.** They process Unit Diary entries and handle day-to-day, but they can't chase your Marines for DTS vouchers or missing training records. That's your job.

3. **Employer notification for AT.** USERRA protects the Marine, but courtesy notice to employers (T-60 minimum) prevents friction. The Marine's responsibility, but leadership should track it.

4. **MROWS lead time is real.** "We'll just submit orders next drill" doesn't work when the system needs 45 days of approval chain. Plan forward.

5. **Medical readiness decays silently.** A Marine can go from green to red between drills because a PHA expired. Check MRRS monthly, not just at drill.
