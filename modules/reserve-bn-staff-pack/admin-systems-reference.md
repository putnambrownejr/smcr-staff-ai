# Reserve Admin Systems Quick Reference (UNCLASSIFIED)

## The Six Platforms Every Reserve Staff Officer Touches

### 1. Drill Manager
- **What it does**: Records IDT attendance, generates pay transactions
- **Who uses it**: Company 1stSgts enter attendance; S-1 reviews/certifies
- **Key rule**: Attendance errors delay the entire pay cycle — get it right the first time
- **Timing**: Must be completed within 5 business days of drill weekend
- **Common problem**: Marines marked present who were actually on ADT orders — double-dipping flags

### 2. MROWS (Marine Reserve Order Writing System)
- **What it does**: Generates ADT, AT, and ADSW orders for reservists
- **Who uses it**: S-1 or designated admin, with approval chain (OIC → CO → higher)
- **Key rule**: Orders must be approved BEFORE the Marine travels — no retroactive orders
- **Timing**: Submit AT orders T-45; ADT at least 2 weeks prior
- **Common problem**: Late submissions → Marines travel without orders → pay/entitlement issues

### 3. DTS (Defense Travel System)
- **What it does**: Travel authorizations and vouchers for all funded travel
- **Who uses it**: Individual Marines create; supervisors/AOs approve
- **Key rule**: Authorization must be approved before travel; voucher within 5 days of return
- **Timing**: Submit authorization T-14 minimum; voucher within 5 business days post-travel
- **Common problem**: Most common post-drill admin drop — Marines forget to file vouchers

### 4. MOL (Marine Online)
- **What it does**: Self-service portal — LES, OMPF, training records, personal info
- **Who uses it**: Every Marine (individual records); S-1 for unit admin functions
- **Key things to check**: LES for pay accuracy, OMPF for promotion package completeness
- **Common problem**: CAC/PKI issues prevent access — have backup auth method ready

### 5. MCTFS / Unit Diary
- **What it does**: Authoritative personnel system — all status changes flow through here
- **Who uses it**: Unit diary clerk (usually I&I admin)
- **Key rule**: If it's not in MCTFS, it didn't happen (promotions, transfers, awards, MOS changes)
- **Common problem**: Diary entries backlogged at I&I → Marine's record doesn't reflect reality

### 6. MRRS / RHRP / PHA
- **What it does**: Medical readiness tracking (MRRS), health risk assessment, periodic health assessments
- **Who uses it**: Medical/dental (data entry); CO/S-1 (readiness reporting)
- **Key rule**: Medical readiness is a retention and deployment gate
- **Common problem**: PHA expiration — Marines fall red without realizing it

## Admin Flow: How These Connect

```
Drill Weekend attendance
        │
        ▼
  Drill Manager (pay)
        │
        ▼
  MROWS (if AT/ADT orders needed)
        │
        ▼
  DTS (travel auth/voucher)
        │
        ▼
  MCTFS/Unit Diary (status changes)
        │
        ▼
  MOL (Marine verifies LES/records)
        │
        ▼
  MRRS (medical readiness current?)
```

## Policy Baselines

| Item | Standard | Reference |
|------|----------|-----------|
| IDT days per year | 48 (24 drill weekends × 2 days) | 10 USC §12332 |
| AT days per year | 12-14 (typically 2 weeks) | MCO 1001.59A |
| IDT travel reimbursement cap | $750/month | MARADMIN 157/25 |
| Drill Manager completion | Within 5 business days of drill | Unit SOP |
| DTS voucher deadline | 5 business days post-travel | JTR |
| MROWS submission (AT) | T-45 days | MARFORRES SOP |
